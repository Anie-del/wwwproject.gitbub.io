Our Ingredients

We source the finest ingredients to create our delightful pastries...

Koeksisters

    We prioritize high-quality ingredients, with a focus on locally-sourced flour and organic milk. Our commitment to excellence extends to every component of this beloved treat.

Anzac Biscuits

    Our commitment to quality is reflected in responsibly sourced oats, coconut, and golden syrup, ensuring the authenticity of this wartime classic.

Empanadas

    Our commitment to authenticity includes sourcing high-quality ingredients. We prioritize locally-sourced meats and fresh produce for the fillings, ensuring a flavorful and sustainable experience.

Croissant

    We source premium flour and butter for an authentic French experience, ensuring the highest quality and taste in every croissant.

Samosa

    We prioritize quality ingredients, sourcing local potatoes and peas, and utilizing ghee for an authentic and flavorful samosa experience.

Bannock

    Our commitment to authenticity involves sourcing quality flour from local mills, ensuring a connection to the land and supporting sustainable practices.

Baklava

    We source premium phyllo dough and locally harvested nuts, ensuring the highest quality. Our honey comes from sustainable and ethical apiaries.

Tiramisu

    We source premium espresso beans, high-quality mascarpone cheese, and organic eggs, ensuring the finest ingredients contribute to the exquisite taste of our tiramisu.
